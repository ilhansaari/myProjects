About Me

Hello! I'm İlhan, an economics student in Turkey. My interest in software began at an early age, and I’ve been working to continually improve myself in this field ever since. Over time, software became a true passion for me. To deepen this passion and build a community around it, I attended a technical high school, where I had the chance to explore different areas and expand my interests. In high school, I gained valuable experience by leading various projects and establishing strong connections with teachers and companies. Blockchain technology, still a new concept at the time, sparked my interest. Seeing how it bridges economics and software motivated me to pursue a place in this field, which ultimately led me to study economics. In my first year at university, I joined the management board of the Blockchain Student Society, allowing me to take a more active role in the industry. Later, with the goal of establishing a global community, I stepped down from the organization but continued to offer software training. Today, I remain focused on developing the community, creating projects in software and economics, writing blogs, and providing training.
